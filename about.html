I started to learn about "Money", Do You Know Money?

I have been learning about money and what continuosly happens to the money systems that were created in the past. We all see where the future is going with technology and bitcoin is perhaps undeniably the 1st mathematically plausible solution that has been able to challenge the current and age old monetary sysytem.

A system that is now so corrupt and damaged that it can really never be fixed. So I decided to do the most I can to help support and "now" help build the future for my children and theirs. I have been providing resources through my twitter accout and recent godaddy website I put together. Follow me and I will leave behind all the resources I can find to help you understand money and see what solutions bitcoin brings that I have learned in my own spiral down the rabbit hole.
